## Pre-Conference Email: In-Person Volunteers

---

Hello Generous Volunteer!

First off, a huge thank you for volunteering! This conference simply wouldn't be possible without amazing people like you who step up to lend a hand and donate your time.

It's important to be punctual for the time slots you've signed up for. Please double-check the role and time you selected by visiting this link: [VOLUNTEER SPREADSHEET LINK]

If for any reason you're unable to make your shift, please let us know as soon as possible so we can try to make other arrangements.

**Key Contacts:**

- General conference: hello@djangocon.us
- General Volunteer: [Volunteer Chairs] on Slack
- Code of Conduct: conduct@djangocon.us or [CoC Chairs] on Slack

If you have not already joined the DjangoCon US Slack, please do so by following this link: [SLACK INVITE LINK]

We'll be holding a quick orientation on the first day of the conference to help you get comfortable with your role and responsibilities. Please come meet us at the registration desk by 8:00 AM on Monday, [DATE], where [NAME] will lead the volunteer orientation. If you can't make it, no worries! Instructions will be provided at your volunteer area, and others will be on hand to guide you.

Make sure to review your responsibilities in our Volunteer Handbook before the event: [HANDBOOK LINK]

Please join the Slack channel: #volunteering-at-dcus

Thank you again so much for volunteering your time to help make this conference happen!
