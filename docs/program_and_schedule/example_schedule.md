# Example schedule

## Tutorial Day

**8:00am** Doors open.

**9:00am** Tutorials (2 or 3)

**12:30pm** Light Lunch.

**1:30pm** Tutorials (2 or 3)

**2:00pm** Swag bag stuffing (volunteer team runs this)

**5:00pm** Wrap-up.

## Talks Day 1

**7:30am** Registration desk opens and breakfast starts

**8:30am** Orientation

**9:00am** Opening remarks (15 min) and keynote (45 min).

**10:00am** 10-minute Sponsored talk (if claimed) or 15-min community update

**10:20am** Break

**10:50am** Talks (2 @ 45 minutes).

**11:40am** Talks (2 @ 25 minutes).

**12:10pm** Lightning Talks (keynote room) / Early Lunch.

**1:00pm** Lunch.

**1:50pm** Talks (2 @ 45 minutes).

**2:40pm** Talks (2 @ 25 minutes).

**3:10pm** Break (Basement).

**3:40pm** Talks (2 @ 45 minutes).

**4:30pm** Talks (2 @ 25 minutes).

**5:00pm** Talks (2 @ 25 minutes).

**5:30pm** End of day 1.

**6:00pm** Opening Party hosted by **Sponsor** at _location_ if offered or board game night at 7 PM

## Talks Day 3 (single-track Deep Dive Day)

- **NOTE** On day 3, you have more flexibility on talks and can easily swap out two 25-minute talks for one 45-minute talk or vice versa depending on what talks best fit the spirit of [Deep Dive Day](https://2019.djangocon.us/news/deep-dive-day/).

**8:00am** Registration desk opens and breakfast starts

**9:00am** Opening remarks (15 min) and keynote (45 min).

**10:00am** 10-minute Sponsored talk (if claimed) or 15-min community update

**10:20am** Break

**10:50am** Talk (45 minutes).

**11:40am** Talk (25 minutes).

**12:10pm** Lightning Talks (keynote room) / Early Lunch.

**1:00pm** Lunch.

**1:50pm** Panel discussion (55 minutes).

- NOTE: Running the panel right after lunch makes setup easier, but there is a slight lull after the talk where we need to reset the stage back to a single-speaker format. There's no obligation to have a panel and you can easily replace it with a 45-minute talk if desired.

**2:55pm** Talk (45 minutes).

**3:45pm** Break

**4:20pm** Talk (25 minutes).

**4:50pm** Talk (45 minutes).

**5:40pm** Closing remarks.

**6:15pm** Tear down talk rooms.
